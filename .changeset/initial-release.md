'smirks': patch

Initial release. Deterministic pixel-face avatars composed of one of 14 eye variants and one of 9 mouth variants, picked from a 32-bit FNV-1a hash of the seed string. Ships with four palettes (`default` soft, `bold`, `monochrome`, `duotone`), independent `fg` / `bg` overrides, and a `currentColor` mode for Tailwind-native theming.
